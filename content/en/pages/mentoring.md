  - heading: "Mentoring: Your Path to Medical School"
    paragraphs:
      - "The transition from the operational field to the academic bench is challenging. The medical school admissions process requires persistence, meticulous preparation, and familiarity with the system. The association's mentoring program connects young members who dream of a white coat with battalion alumni who have already walked the path successfully. Our mentors offer personal guidance including:"
    items:
      - "Guidance and counseling: for entrance exams, psychometric tests, and admissions interviews."
      - "Sharing personal experience: tips for coping with pressure and choosing the right study track in Israel or abroad."
      - 'A listening ear: "big brother" or "big sister" mentorship from someone who understands exactly where you come from and where you aspire to go.'
  - heading: "Networking & Career Development: Opening Doors in Healthcare"
    paragraphs:

    items:
      - "Department placement: direct connection to senior physicians and managers among battalion alumni, assisting with introductions, recommendations, and placement in residencies and specific departments."
      - "Professional consulting: consultation on choosing a specialty, sub-specialties in Israel and abroad, and research tracks."
      - "Industry connections: networking that connects medical professionals, entrepreneurs, and technologists from among our alumni, for integration into the biomedical and digital health worlds."
